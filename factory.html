Our Factory

All Studioforma manufacturing is done in India. We take pride in our Indian heritage and employ hundreds of local individuals who contribute to our growth and craftsmanship.

Built on Realply Industries

Studioforma is backed by Realply Industries, our parent company in raw materials and wood-based manufacturing. This vertical integration means we control quality from raw material to finished surface.

Quality Standards

  • E1 Grade glue across all door construction
  • Flush door construction engineered for high-traffic spaces
  • Strict quality checks for consistency across bulk orders
  • Thickness options from 30 mm to 50 mm for every application
  • Thick Recon and Natural Veneer processing in-house

Capacity for Scale

Our Project Series is built for large-scale residential, commercial, and hospitality projects. Each door undergoes strict quality checks to ensure consistency and reliability across bulk orders, with easy installation and maintenance designed in from the start.
