Experiences

Every Studioforma surface is a material story. From rich natural veneers to high-gloss UV coatings, our finishes are chosen to bring texture, character, and warmth into a space.

Finishes and Materials

  • Natural Veneer: real wood character, no two panels alike.
  • Thick Recon Veneer: consistent grain, engineered for uniformity across large projects.
  • UV Coating: high-gloss, scratch-resistant surfaces with a sleek modern look.
  • PU Paint: smooth painted finishes in matte or gloss, protected from moisture and daily wear.
  • Laminate: timeless textures and finishes, durable and easy to maintain.
