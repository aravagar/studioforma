About Us

Get to Know Us

Our wood-veneered furniture adds elegance and style to your home. We offer a variety of options for both residential and commercial spaces, allowing you to create your ideal environment. Choosing Studioforma furniture will enhance the overall look of your home.

One of the key features that stands out is the door. It is the first thing people notice and sets the tone for your style. Choose wisely from our selection of beautiful wood-veneered designs.

How We Stand Out

Our wood-veneered pieces are not only visually appealing but also budget-friendly, allowing you to fulfill your dreams without breaking the bank. We offer a wide range of products designed to enhance the vibe and glamour of your space.

Three Decades of Experience

We draw on 30 years of experience in the furniture industry to create chic, exciting, and elegant designs. We take pride in our Indian heritage and employ hundreds of local individuals to contribute to our growth. All our manufacturing is done in India, and we are proudly Indian.

Happy to Serve

Our head office is located in New Delhi, and we serve clients throughout India, ensuring a strong national presence. Please reach out to us with your requirements, and we will handle everything from there.
